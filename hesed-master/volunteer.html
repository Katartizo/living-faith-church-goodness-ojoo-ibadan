Join a Service Unit

Serving God is a privilege and a guaranteed pathway to divine blessings. At Living Faith Church Goodness, Ojoo, we believe that every member has a unique gift given by God to contribute to the growth of the church. "And ye shall serve the LORD your God, and he shall bless thy bread, and thy water..." (Exodus 23:25).

Join a service unit today and become an active part of the Liberation Mandate. Whether you love to sing, have a passion for keeping the house of God clean, or enjoy welcoming new people, there is a perfect place for you to express your gifts and be a blessing to the congregation.


Serve With Us Through the Following Units

Ushers & Crowd Control.

The welcoming face of the church. This unit is responsible for maintaining order, directing worshippers to their seats, organizing the congregation during ministrations, and ensuring a peaceful atmosphere during all services.

Children's Church.

Dedicated to raising the next generation of giants. This unit teaches the Word of Faith to children in a fun, engaging, and age-appropriate manner, ensuring they grow in the way of the Lord.

The Choir.

Ministering to God and the congregation through high praises and deep worship. The choir creates an atmosphere for the miraculous to take place through anointed and spirited musical renditions.

Prayer Squad.

The spiritual engine room of the church. This unit stands in the gap, interceding for the church leadership, the congregation, the success of our services, and the advancement of the Kingdom of God.

Protocol & Hospitality.

This unit ensures that visiting ministers, special guests, and new converts are well taken care of. They handle logistics, welfare, and ensure everyone feels the love of Christ upon entering the church.

Sanctuary Keepers.

Cleanliness is next to godliness. This dedicated team ensures that the church auditorium, premises, and conveniences are kept spotless, neat, and conducive for worship at all times.

Technical & Media.

Handling all audio, visual, and lighting operations. This unit ensures excellent sound quality in the auditorium and manages the live streaming of services to our online audience across the globe.

Decoration Unit.

Responsible for the beautification of the sanctuary. This creative team uses floral arrangements and stage designs to make the House of God glorious and welcoming for every service.
