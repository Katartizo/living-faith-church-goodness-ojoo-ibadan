We Are Winners

Living Faith Church Worldwide, also known as Winners' Chapel International, is a global network of churches with a mandate to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the Word of Faith. Here at the Goodness Branch in Ojoo, Ibadan, we are raising a people of power, purpose, and prosperity who command dominion in all spheres of life.

The Liberation Mandate.

Our journey began with a divine encounter. On May 2, 1981, God gave His servant, Bishop David Oyedepo, an eighteen-hour heavenly vision. God said: "And from the beginning it was not so... and now, the hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil, through the preaching of the Word of Faith; and I am sending you to undertake this task." Today, testimonies of healing, deliverance, and breakthroughs validate this calling globally.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to see believers walk in total victory in every area of their lives. Through the undiluted teaching of the Word of God, we empower individuals to discover their divine heritage, walk in the miraculous, and dominate their world. We are committed to spiritual growth, kingdom service, and raising a triumphant army of believers who live completely above the struggles and limitations of the natural realm.

"For whatsoever is born of God overcometh the world: and this is the victory that overcometh the world, even our faith."

1 John 5:4 KJV

Our Pillars of Faith.

The Word of God

The Bible is our manual for profitable living. We believe that the Word of God is the foundation for every triumph in life. It is the compass that guides our destiny and the ultimate authority over every life situation.

Faith

Faith is the universal currency for all earthly transactions. We teach that without faith, it is impossible to please God. Our faith is built strictly on what the Word of God says, empowering us to move mountains and subdue kingdoms.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the power, baptism, and daily manifestation of the Holy Spirit. He is our Comforter, our Guide, and the source of our supernatural strength to do exploits in our generations.

The Supernatural (Signs and Wonders)

We are a people of signs and wonders. We believe that Christianity without the supernatural is a mere religion. Expect miracles, divine health, and undeniable breakthroughs as a lifestyle in Christ.

Wisdom

Divine wisdom is the principal thing. We believe in the practical application of the Word of God to produce desired, undeniable results in every sphere of life.

Success

Success is our covenant heritage. We are redeemed to be the head and not the tail, excelling in our careers, businesses, and all life endeavors by the power of God.

Divine Healing

Divine healing is the children's bread. We believe that by His stripes we were healed, and we are entitled to walk in total health, vitality, and freedom from all sicknesses.

Praise

Praise is God’s dwelling place. We teach that through high praises, we invoke the presence of God to execute judgment upon our enemies and command fearful blessings.


What We Believe.

Vision & Purpose.

We believe every individual is created with a unique divine assignment. Through the light of the Word, we help believers discover, pursue, and fulfill their God-given visions, ensuring they live a life of maximum impact.

Prayer & Fasting.

Prayer is not just a religious activity; it is our communication line to Heaven. Coupled with fasting, it is a spiritual weapon for securing speedy answers, breaking yokes, and commanding the supernatural.

Prosperity.

God takes pleasure in the prosperity of His servants. We believe that through covenant practices like tithing, kingdom investments, and diligent work, believers are empowered for financial dominion.

Consecration.

Purity is the foundation for lasting power. We believe in living a life of holiness and dedication to God, ensuring our vessels are always ready and fit for the Master's use in every area of life.
