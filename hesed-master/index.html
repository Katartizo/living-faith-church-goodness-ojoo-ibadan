Welcome to Winners' Chapel

Welcome to Living Faith Church Worldwide (Winners' Chapel International), Goodness branch, Ojoo, Ibadan. We are a family of believers dedicated to teaching the Word of Faith, experiencing the power of God, and spreading the Liberation Mandate to our community.
