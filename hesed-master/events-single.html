Special Anniversary Thanksgiving Service

Join us this Sunday for a momentous celebration as we mark a special milestone in God's faithfulness at our Special Anniversary Thanksgiving Service. The Bible declares in Psalm 118:15, "The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tabernacles of the righteous." This Sunday, we pause to honour the journey God has brought us through — every step, every season, and every story of His grace that has carried us to this anniversary.

Come ready to reflect, rejoice, and give thanks. Because it is our Special Anniversary Thanksgiving, make sure you come dressed in your finest and with a heart overflowing with gratitude! We will be lifting up a collective offering of praise to God for His unwavering faithfulness, His covering over our lives, and the legacy of grace He continues to write through this commission. Come along with your entire family, your friends, and your loved ones. This anniversary belongs to all of us — let us celebrate it together in glory!
