Covenant Day of Vengeance

Join us this Sunday for a highly prophetic service, our Covenant Day of Vengeance. The Bible declares in Isaiah 63:4, "For the day of vengeance is in mine heart, and the year of my redeemed is come." This Sunday, God is arising in His anger to judge every oppressor of your destiny, health, and family. Every long-standing battle, spiritual attack, and invisible barrier will face the wrath of God. When God takes vengeance, your total liberty is guaranteed!

Come expectant and ready to engage your faith. We will be invoking the mysteries of the Kingdom to execute judgment against the camp of the enemy. Come along with your families, your written expectations, and anyone who needs supernatural deliverance. The siege over your life is finally over, and you are returning with your undeniable testimonies!
