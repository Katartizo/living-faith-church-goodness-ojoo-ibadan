Covenant Family Day

Join us this Sunday for a glorious time in God's presence at our Covenant Family Day & End of the Month Thanksgiving Service. The Bible declares in Psalm 118:15, "The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tabernacles of the righteous." This Sunday, God is set to visit your household, restore peace, and pour out His undeniable blessings upon your family. Every siege of the enemy over your home is broken, and a new season of joy is beginning!

Come expectant and ready to celebrate. Because it is our End of the Month Thanksgiving, make sure you come with your dancing shoes and a heart full of gratitude! We will be returning all the glory to God for His preservation throughout the month, which will automatically secure our blessings for the month ahead. Come along with your entire family, your friends, and your loved ones. Your family is next in line for a turnaround testimony!
