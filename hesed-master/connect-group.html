Winners Satellite Fellowship

The Winners Satellite Fellowship (WSF) is our house-to-house fellowship platform. It is a place where we share the Word of God, pray for one another, and experience true family love and community support in a more intimate setting.

We have various WSF centers located across Ojoo and its environs, meeting every Saturday from 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Find a center near you to grow spiritually, connect with other believers, and be a part of our caring church family. We are our brother's keeper!
